Meet Johny. Johny is a lovable fella. He is living the American Dream - has a condo, a car, a good job and a loving fiancee he refers to as "my future wife". Together they go on dates where they drink scotchka, eat pizza and do other stuff.

It is a date night tonight, but unless Johny completes three tasks, he will get no sweet lovin'. He must pick all the spoons from the floor of his apartment, then go to work and get a promotion and later buy a dozen red roses.
