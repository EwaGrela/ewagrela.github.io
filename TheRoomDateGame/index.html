Date Night

Meet Johny. Johny is a lovable fella. He is living the American Dream - has a condo, a car, a good job and a loving fiancee he refers to as "my future wife".
Together they go on dates where they drink scotchka, eat pizza and do other stuff.
Weren't it for his enemies - Chris-R and Claudette - his life would be perfect.

It is a date night tonight, but unless Johny completes three tasks, he will get no sweet lovin'.
He must pick the spoons from the floor of his apartment, then get a promotion and later buy a dozen red roses.
